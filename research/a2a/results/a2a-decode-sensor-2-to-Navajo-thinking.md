This is Navajo way of holding this message:
---
This is a report. It comes from the thing that feels movement.

This is only one single breath, one instant. No trail of earlier readings travels with it. No pattern of what comes next is here.

It speaks of three directions it feels push. It speaks of three directions it feels turn. It tells its own living condition. It tells how far it leans, two ways.
The downward pull sits exactly as it should sit, when nothing moves. All other pushes, all other turns, rest almost perfectly quiet. Only the very faintest slow turn is present, you would not even feel it.

It marks itself as working good. No boundary has been crossed. No broken thing lives here.

There is no hurry. No one needs to run. No one needs call anyone. This is not a warning. This is not a request. This is just the thing breathing out its state, as it does when all is as it should be.

It does not say how often it looks. It does not say how sure its words are. It does not give its own name. It speaks only with the agreed marks that all these things use when they speak.

Beneath all the numbers, this is all it really comes to say:
*I am well. I am sitting still. Nothing is wrong.*
