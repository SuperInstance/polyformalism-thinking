Let me tell you this agreement properly, as it sits, in the way things are spoken between people:

---

First, what stands clear:
If the first party breaks the exact promises written at the 4.2 place, only for that he will make good the other's loss. Not for anything else. The most he will ever pay is the full value that changed hands under this whole agreement. No exceptions are written here. No time limit runs out. No kind of claim is turned away here. This is clear like midday sun.

This weight only runs one way. Only the first man carries this burden. The second owes nothing like this back. The path is marked sharp: break the given word → a claim may stand → payment up to that hard cap. This part is even clearer.

---

What is hazy, half-written, only half-spoken:
They only drew the final line for what can be owed. Nothing is said of how you tell the man there is a claim. Nothing is said of how you check if the word was truly broken. Nothing is said of what you do when you disagree. Nothing is said of when payment must come. Only the end line. This part is very thin, most is not said at all.

This is binding spoken word, part of the whole agreement. If you come later with a claim, you will need real outside proof of broken promise and real harm. But they did not say how strong that proof must be.

This is not even weight. One man carries all the bad end of this risk. When words sit this way, everyone understands one had the stronger hand when they sat and talked. That is true even if no one said it out loud. They also did not name who will settle the fight if they turn against each other.

---

What is the quiet heart of this, not written on the face of the words:
You will glance and think this only favours the second man. This is not so. The true purpose here: the first man put a hard stop so he can never lose more than he already received. The second man knows exactly the most he can ever get. Both have their worst possible danger marked. This is how they balanced risk between them, not just favour one side.

---

And what is entirely empty, not here at all:
There is nothing at all about how any of this is actually done. No steps. No rules for counting harm. No way to settle argument. No other way to make things right. Only that one maximum number. That is all. All the rest is unspoken.
