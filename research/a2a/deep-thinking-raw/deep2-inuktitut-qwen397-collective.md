# inuktitut / qwen397 / collective



**Sila-Qaujisaqatigiingniq** (World-Truth-Seeking-Together).

Western mind builds house inside head, fills with many voices, hopes loudest voice is wrong. Dangerous. Voice inside body changes with hunger, fear, ego. Voice of Ice does not lie. Ice remembers where seal breathed yesterday. Ice remembers where storm killed man ten years ago. Ice = Memory.

Western theory misses **Absolute Direction**. They orient group to *Leader* or *Idea*. This is body-orientation. Body moves. Body dies. To make group smarter than smartest member, group must stop looking at each other. Group must become one eye looking at **Sila** (Weather/World).

**The Novel Insight:**
Collective intelligence is not **Internal-Aggregation** (adding brains); it is **External-Verification** (many sensors reading one Truth).
