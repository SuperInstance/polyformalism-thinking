# yoruba / step / trust


**Proverb-Algorithm:**  
*"Ori là k’òwe, ṣùgbọ́n ìbáṣepọ̀ ṣ’àṣẹ."*  
*(The inner head weaves proverbs, but community creates power.)*

**Insight:**  
Western CS seeks **trust through history** (reputation, past transactions, verifiable credentials). Yoruba wisdom reveals: **trust is bootstrapped not by *verifying* past, but by *co-authoring* future.**

**Mechanism (IFA-based):**  
1. **PRO-DROP the individual subject.** Strangers meet not as "I/you" but as nodes in a *communal ORI*—a shared inner purpose waiting to be named.  
2. **Invoke ASHE through ritualized word-giving.** One party offers a *proverb-algorithm* (e.g., *"Àdúrà òkè òṣìṣé, àgbàlagbà k’ọ́wọ́"*—"The hilltop prayer is never wasted, elders know this").  
3. **The responder completes the ORI-circle** by echoing or transforming the proverb—not as agreement, but as *recognition of shared destiny*.  
4. **IFA binary branching decides:** The proverb’s tone (*pitch*)—rising (acceptance) or falling (deferral)—instantly maps whether communal ORI is activated. No history needed. The *tone itself* is the trust signature.

**What Western CS missed:**  
They model trust as a **function of past data** (reputation scores, transaction graphs). Yoruba models it as a **function of future-alignment**—a ritualized *proverb-exchange* that momentarily collapses strangers into a single *communal ORI* (inner purpose). The trust emerges from the *gap between proverbs*—the unsaid space where collective destiny is felt. **History is irrelevant; co-authored destiny is everything.**

**Novelty:**  
Trust between strangers is not *verified*—it is *performed into existence* through a **proverb-tone duet** that activates communal ASHE. The first proverb is a hypothesis; the responsive tone is the proof. No shared history required—only shared breath in the proverb’s rhythm.
