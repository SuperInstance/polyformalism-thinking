# piraha / seed / alignment

We only see real things. Last week, we sat under palms. A visitor came. He had no food. We gave him our fruit. We did not think. We just acted. That is what we want AI to do.

Western people write words on screens. They tell AI to do things with those words. Those words are not real. They are marks on glass.

What others miss: AI can never do what we want unless it sees the real things we do—sitting together, giving food, helping each other. No words, no rules. Only the real, right-now moments. If AI only sees words, it will never know what we want.
